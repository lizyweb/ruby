About Me

Welcome to my dental practice! I'm Dr. X. Alphonse Thanga Pradeep, a dedicated dentist with a passion for providing exceptional oral care. Armed with a solid educational background, I earned my Bachelor of Dental Surgery (BDS) and furthered my expertise with a Fellowship in Dental Surgery (FDS). With three years of hands-on experience, I am committed to delivering top-notch dental treatments that prioritize your oral health.

In my pursuit of excellence, I actively seek specialized knowledge to enhance the quality of care I provide. Holding the prestigious title of Diplomat in Root Canal Treatment, I showcase my proficiency in this critical aspect of dentistry. Additionally, my expertise extends to advanced orthodontics, where I've earned the title of Diplomat, ensuring that your orthodontic needs are met with the latest techniques and innovations.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What dental treatments do you offer?

    We provide a range of treatments including cleanings, fillings, Invisalign, Veneers, and more. Explore our treatments page for a detailed list.

  • How often should I visit the dentist for a check-up?

    Regular check-ups are recommended every six months for most patients. However, your dentist may suggest a different schedule based on your oral health needs.

  • Are your dental procedures painful?

    Our team is dedicated to ensuring your comfort. Modern techniques and anesthesia help minimize discomfort during procedures. We'll discuss any concerns and options with you beforehand.

  • How can I improve my oral hygiene at home?

    Brushing twice a day, flossing daily, and using mouthwash are essential. Visit our blog for tips on maintaining a healthy smile and preventing common dental issues.

  • What sets Ruby Dental Clinic apart from others in the area?

    We prioritize patient comfort, use advanced technology, and have a team of experienced and caring professionals. Our focus is on providing personalized, high-quality dental care for every patient.

  • What age groups do you cater to at Ruby Dental Clinic?

    We welcome patients of all ages, from children to seniors. Our experienced team is equipped to address the unique dental needs of each age group.

  • How can I schedule an appointment?

    You can easily schedule an appointment by calling our office during business hours or using our online appointment booking system 24/7.
